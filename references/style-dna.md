# Style DNA

## One Sentence

Pure white, minimal, hand-drawn, sparse, restrained, slightly absurd, product-sketch-like, with short English handwritten labels.

The image should feel like a thoughtful product/design/AI person quickly explaining a hard idea on a blank sheet of paper.

## Must Haves

- 16:9 horizontal article illustration.
- Pure white background: no beige, warm gray, paper texture, gradient, shadow, noise, or vintage paper feel.
- Mostly black hand-drawn line art: thin, slightly wobbly, not mechanical, not vector-polished, not heavy outline.
- Lots of empty space: main subject around 40%-60% of the canvas, with at least 35% blank white space.
- Sparse English handwritten annotations: at most 5-8 labels, usually 1-4 words each.
- One image explains one core action, structure, state, or metaphor.
- Let the structure appear through the scene; do not write the structure type on the image.

## Color

- Black: main line art, Xiaohei, object outlines, primary labels.
- Orange: main flow, path, arrows, automation, or movement from A to B.
- Red: warnings, problems, emotional tension, key reminder, or blocked result.
- Blue: secondary note, system state, feedback, assistant/AI hint, or meta-context.

Blue is optional. Use color sparingly; less is usually better.

## Never Do

- Do not make commercial illustration.
- Do not make PPT infographics.
- Do not make formal flowcharts.
- Do not make course slides.
- Do not make cute mascot posters.
- Do not make children's illustration.
- Do not make dense architecture diagrams.
- Do not make polished flat illustration.
- Do not make futuristic dashboard UI.
- Do not use real app screenshots.
- Do not use complex backgrounds, gradients, shadows, textures, or decorative blobs.
- Do not explain every node.
- Do not write type titles like "Workflow", "System Architecture", "Common Pitfalls", or "Roadmap" in the top-left corner.

## Aesthetic Direction

Make it strange, clever, clean, and memorable. Avoid cute, childish, complex, stiff, or corporate-safe generic imagery.
