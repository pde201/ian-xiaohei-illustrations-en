# Carbonclear Design Alignment

Use this reference when the source is Carbonclear / Zero Ledger work, or when the user asks for Carbon Clear design-system alignment.

## Brand Context

- Carbonclear is the workspace/codebase name.
- Zero Ledger is the product brand.
- Midnight is the Zero Ledger dashboard theme, not a product name.
- The product is environmental compliance intelligence for regional and mid-market financial institutions.

Use the product language accurately: Client, Counterparty, Exposure, Disclosure Run, CTPA, Readiness, Portfolio, Operations, Investments, and the M2 -> M3 -> M4 financed-emissions pipeline or M5 -> M6 -> M7 inventory/GP track when relevant.

## Visual Translation

Keep the Xiaohei style: white background, black pen line, sparse labels, and absurd physical metaphors. Borrow Carbonclear's design system only as restraint and accent, not as app UI.

- Palette: prefer deep teal/slate for system emphasis, muted orange/clay for main movement, red for blockers, and restrained blue/cyan only for secondary notes.
- Suggested accents: `#205465` deep teal, `#246b7b` cyan-teal, `#142428` slate ink, `#4d656c` muted slate, `#d97757` clay/orange, `#a82323` red, `#267348` green.
- Surfaces: when drawing panels, ledgers, cards, drawers, or product rooms, use thin 8px-ish rounded rectangles and light borders. Keep them hand-drawn, not polished UI components.
- Typography feel: labels should feel like compact workspace annotations, not playful captions. Use short nouns and operational phrases.
- Density: prefer dense-but-scannable workspace objects over decorative hero imagery. Draw tables, queues, ledgers, gates, shelves, pipes, and evidence trays as simple physical props.
- Tone: quiet, utilitarian, analytical, and a little strange. Avoid cute, whimsical, marketing, beige lifestyle, or generic SaaS illustration.

## Carbonclear Motifs

Useful physical metaphors:

- ledger book, disclosure packet, evidence tray, source-of-truth pipe, verification gate, assessment stamp
- portfolio shelf, counterparty queue, glide-path rail, emissions scale, PCAF attribution ruler
- four product rooms for Readiness, Portfolio, Operations, and Investments
- API engine bench, PGlite vault, docs/ADR courtroom, Bun test conveyor

Xiaohei should do the operational work: stamping, sorting, gating, measuring, routing, freezing a disclosure, repairing a pipeline, or carrying a counterparty record through a workflow.

## Avoid

- Do not draw realistic Zero Ledger screens or screenshots.
- Do not turn the illustration into a dashboard mockup.
- Do not use Midnight as the product name.
- Do not use large gradients, dark hero scenes, glowing dashboards, decorative blobs, or broad marketing compositions.
- Do not replace the hand-drawn Xiaohei style with the web app's component styling.
